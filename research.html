Ongoing Projects

Drop–bath impacts

Explores how drops interact with deep and shallow baths, focusing on rebound, coalescence, cavity dynamics, and air entrainment under varying ambient conditions and substrate deformability.

Water entry

Investigates the entry of solid and liquid bodies into a fluid bath, uncovering how ambient gas, entry velocity, and surface properties influence cavity formation and seal dynamics.

Liquid sheets and bells

Studies the formation, breakup, and stability of axisymmetric liquid sheets and bell-like structures formed under drop or jet impact on surfaces or into liquid pools.

Granular interfaces

Examines the collective dynamics and stability of particle-laden liquid interfaces, focusing on deformation-induced collapse, jamming, and spreading under capillary and mechanical forcing.
