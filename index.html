Chase T. Gabbard

Hope Street Postdoctoral Fellow – Brown University

Hello, and welcome to my homepage. I am a Hope Street Postdoctoral Fellow at Brown University, where I study problems in fluid mechanics. My research centers on multiphase flows, interfacial phenomena, and capillary-scale dynamics. I am particularly interested in how liquid drops and beads interact with fibers, non-wetting surfaces, and liquid baths—systems that offer insight into how small droplets spread, rebound, or are captured. These behaviors have important implications for droplet transport, collection, and control. You can learn more about my current and past work on the Research page.
